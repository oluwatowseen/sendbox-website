Sendbox allows businesses to schedule hundreds of packages for delivery in simple steps.
Say goodbye to the hassle of manual tracking and poor customer service so you can

Safe & Trusted

Access to a wider network

Our partner network gives you access to a wide selection of logistics/delivery partners coupled with their best rates to choose from.

Break shipping barriers. Ship local & International

Don’t let your location limit the number of customers your business serves anymore. By being connected to the Sendbox partner network, you can even do door to door deliveries to various locations around the world.

Save & Get Rewarded!

Compared with conventional shipping options, processing shipments through Sendbox is less costly. Our partnership with logistics companies saves you more funds. In addition, you can get upto 50% discount when you process 500 shipments or more in a month using Sendbox.

Pay on Delivery Service

Don’t worry anymore about customers that insist on receiving goods before they make payments. Through our logistics partners, Sendbox will help you collect the fees for your goods and remit same to you.

Stand-alone online merchant stores are also covered

Sendbox API integration feature takes away the stress of having to fill customer details one by one. Stand-alone merchant stores on Magento, and woo commerce platforms are supported.

Ship with assurance

While on transit, if anything goes wrong with your shipment, you’re absolutely covered. Your shipments are fully covered by insurance. Logistics partners on Sendbox are trusted brands.
