Sendbox for Individuals

Sendbox is easy to use

You can start sending packages in 3 easy steps.
1. Setup your account. Don't worry, it's free. You only pay for what you ship.
2. Tell us where you want the package picked up and delivered, a description of the shipment and anything else that's important.
3. Send your request and we handle the rest. Sit back, relax and track your delivery.

You only pay for what you ship.

There are no monthly subscriptions, contracts or minimum order restrictions.

Save when you ship through Sendbox

Compared with conventional shipping options, processing shipments through Sendbox is less costly. Our partnership with logistics companies gives you the best rate across different carriers.

Ship with assurance

While on transit, if anything goes wrong with your shipment, you’re absolutely covered. Your shipments are fully covered by insurance. Logistics partners on Sendbox are trusted brands.

Pay on Delivery Service

Don’t worry anymore about customers that insist on receiving goods before they make payments. Through our logistics partners, Sendbox will help you collect the fees for your goods and remit same to you.
